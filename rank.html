ADMIN

  • Experience creating plugins, texture packs, and builds, with a passion for creating content related to the game and lobby.
  • Never having received any penalties from the server (including mutes/bans, and including the Discord server).
  • Ability to communicate effectively and frequently within the team.
  • Ability to behave in a manner that does not cause discomfort to other players.
  • Ability to use a high-performance, noise-free microphone.
  • Only those who meet the above conditions should apply.
  • An interview (Discord voice call) may be required. (Camera off / Microphone on)
