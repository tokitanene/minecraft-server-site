Server Rules

Using prohibited mods will result in punishment. (This applies to both current and past usage.)

Administrators can verify which mods and textures you are using.

If you believe you have been punished in error, please join the Discord server, create an Appeal a Punishment ticket, enter your username, and wait. An administrator will address your case as soon as possible.

If you are banned from the server, the reason for the ban will be listed on the Discord server alongside your username. This is visible to all players.

Administrators may impose penalties for any mods deemed inappropriate or cheating, even if they are not listed below.

Chat and Voicechat Rules ▶
  • No harassment, hate speech, or discrimination.
  • No spamming or excessive caps.
  • No advertising without staff permission.
  • Use appropriate language in public channels.
Gameplay Rules ▶
  • No exploiting bugs or unintended mechanics.
  • No teaming where it is not allowed.
  • Play fair and respect competitive integrity.
Disallowed Mods ▶
    Any resource pack or mod that alters the opacity of a block that would normally not be transparent is prohibited.
  • X-Ray mods or texture packs
  • Clear Lava mods or texture packs
    • Mod that reveal enemy player locations are prohibited.
  • Minimap (With and without entities)
    • The use of mods that alter player movement is prohibited.
  • Reach
  • Fly hacks
    • The use of mods that automate player movement is prohibited.
  • Mouse Tweaks
  • Auto-miners
  • Auto click
  • Aim Assist
